About Us

Hi I’m Billy. I opened my first butcher shop, Meat at Billy’s here in 2009. This site has been a working butcher shop dating back to the 1970’s. Back then it was one of four butcher shops in the block. How times have changed!

We’re one of the few butcher shops left who order in whole carcasses and break them down ourselves. This allows us to offer our customers a wide variety of fresh cuts. As a result we get many dedicated home cooks searching us out to track down those lesser known cuts of meat.

We take pride in the quality of our meat and offer what we consider to be, some of the best steaks in town! We’re well known for our sausages too. They’re made here of course and all 20 flavours are gluten free and made from fresh ingredients.

In the last few years we’ve noticed increasing demand for high quality, free- range local meat and our product lines have expanded accordingly. We now stock both grain and grass-fed beef, QLD Gooralie free- range pork products, organic chickens and free- range ducks.

We're butchers who love to cook and more importantly, love to EAT! We love coming up with ideas and recipes for our ready to cook range - ideal for busy people. Try our marinated butterflied legs of lamb. They're great on the Weber! We also offer stuffed pork belly roasts, the tastiest burger patties in town, creamy chicken & pumpkin parcels, hickory glazed pork spare ribs and our now famous - pesto lamb backstraps.

We're more than just a butcher shop. We also have a great deli section where you can find anything from marinades, sauces and vinegars to cous cous, preserved lemons, chipotle peppers and master stock. In our display fridge you’ll find local and imported cheeses, paté, cream and butter too!

If you’re in the neighbourhood, drop in and say hi! We’d love to meet you.
