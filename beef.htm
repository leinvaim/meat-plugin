Blicks River Beef

Tender grass-fed beef raised in a unique natural environment, high up on the New England Tablelands. This cool climate area includes some of the most spectacular, rugged and remote wilderness in Australia and is ideal grazing country for young cattle.

Nolan Meat Private Selection

Private Selection is produced from grain finished Yearling Beef, renowned for superior eating quality and is characterised by a clean juicy taste, which on eating, delivers a light crisp flavour on the palate. Private Selection Beef is hung by the tenderstretch method to enhance tenderness.

www.nolan.com.au

Robbins Island - Full Blood, Grassfed Wagyu

John, Keith and Chauncey Hammond have been selectively breeding Japanese Full-Blood Wagyu on Robbins Island (Tasmania) since the early 1990’s. Situated on the North Western tip of Tasmania, the prized cattle are grazed naturally and sustainably on lush pastures without supplements or hormones, all while breathing the cleanest air on the planet.

www.robbinsislandwagyu.com.au

 

Diamantina Wagyu (Qld)

Diamantina Wagyu is sourced from the finest Japanese Wagyu genetics available.  They're grazed naturally for the majority of their lives before moving to carefully prepared rations including Australian wheat and barley. It is this unique combination of the best breeds, a pristine environment and careful rearing that result in its exceptional marbling, tenderness and flavour sought out by the finest restaurants around the world.

www.stanbroke.com.au/diamantina-beef
