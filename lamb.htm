New England Gold

New England Gold is an award winning lamb product sourced from the vast lush green pastures of the New England region, NSW. Originally colonised in the early 1800s, the New England region has a prestigious farming and gold mining history, with some of Australia’s most successful agricultural companies refining the art of lamb breeding and finishing over hundreds of years.

 A hormone and anti-biotic free product, New England Gold lamb has eating quality assured through the Meat Standards Australia (MSA) grading program. A paddock to plate system, MSA provides a guaranteed eating experience through quality control of the entire production chain.

Sovereign Lamb

Sovereign Lamb is all natural prime lamb raised in the pristine Goldfields regions of Victoria Australia. This region was home to the Gold rush from 1835-1900 and is now considered to be a "natural haven", famous for its rich, fertile and productive land, it is some of the finest lamb producing country in the world.

The pure lamb genetics of the Sovereign Lamb and the natural feeding regime of lush pastures, clean water and air, give the lamb a tender texture and a mild flavour, unlike any other. This is gourmet lamb of the highest quality; every cut is outstanding, from the elegant rack of lamb to the boneless leg roast.
