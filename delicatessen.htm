Delicatessen Products


 

We're more than just a butcher shop and that's why 'foodies' love Meat @ Billy’s! We also stock a great range deli & everyday grocery items. We do our best to support locally made product where we can too.

 

If you’re having trouble sourcing a rare ingredient, please ask us. If we don’t stock it, we can generally point you in the right direction!
