More than a butcher shop!

 

Meat at Billy’s strives to be what the old neighbourhood butcher shop used to be - a friendly place with consistent, top quality products coupled with good old fashioned service, all done with a smile. Free cheerios for the kids, we open the door for you and are more than happy to carry your purchases to your car if you need us to.

 

We're best known for our great steaks and proudly offer 'Certified Black Angus Beef' in both grain-finish or pure grass-fed options. Some of our customers even travel from the Southside and outer Brisbane for it. It's that good!
